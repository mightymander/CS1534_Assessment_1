Scalloway Fire Festival

Date: January

The origins of the Fire Festival, the tradition began at the begging of the 1900's. The event was held on Christmas Day. This continued until 1979 where the name officially became know as the Scalloway Fire Festival. In 1984 the date was moved to the second friday in January.

The day of The Fire Festival, During the day the Jarl squad visits local schools during the day time. After the torchlit procession through the night, walking through the whole village. Next to the boating club beside the sea,The gally is burnt and sent into the sea

After the main spectacle, the Squads visit 5 local halls: Bridge End, Hamnavoe, Tingwall, Scalloway Hall, Scalloway Legion and Scalloway Boating Club. For a night filled with dancing and fun.

A key difference is that in the Scalloway Fire Festival Men and Women take part together in the procession

Scalloway Castle

Address: Castle St, Scalloway, Shetland ZE1 0TP

Scalloway Castle, was the home of Patrick Stewart, a horrible man. He became known as the 'Black Patie', he was known for his notorious oppression. He was accused of using forced labour in Scalloway's construction in 1599. There was a happy ending for him though, he was executed in Edinburgh in 1615

The castle itself stands as a sophisticated and impressive example of the marvelous architecture from the 1500's

Everytime you go past it, its a reminder of Patrick Stewart's harsh rule.

Scalloway Museum

Address: Castle St, Scalloway, Shetland ZE1 0TP

Scalloway museum first opened in 1985, by the Scalloway History Group. One of its main themes was 'The Shetland Bus' the nickname given to a clandestine and dangerous operation carried out across the North Sea between Shetland and occupied Norway during World War 2, which we will talk about next

After 20 years of the museum being opened and run by the Scalloway History Group, it was in need of some work it had outgrown its old premises. The society instigated a six-year project to fund a create the museum in the photo to your left.

The new museum was opened in 2012 by tge Prime Minster of Norway. Its one of the most popular attractions for visitors when coming to Scalloway.

Shetland Weight Training Club

Address: Ladysmith Rd, Scalloway, Shetland ZE1 0XD

Shetland Weight Training Club (SWTC) is run by its members for its members! It is is a small team of gym addicts trying to create the best training space for other gym addicts.

Its open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year

Every year they host a powerlifting competition and a strongman competition. Anyone from the public is welcome to go and watch the feats of strengths accomplished.

Scalloway Pool

Address: New Rd, Scalloway, Shetland ZE1 0TN

Scalloway pool was opened April 4th 1993, and is right next to Scalloway Primary School. The leisure facilities are offered to all.

The pool consists of a four lane, 16.7m main pool, a jacuzzi, a kids pool and a steam room
